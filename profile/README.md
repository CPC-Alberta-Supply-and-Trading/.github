## About Us

Capital Power Supply and Trading operates as part of Capital Power Corporation (TSX: CPX), a growth-oriented North American power producer headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta.

## Our Alberta Operations

We manage a diverse portfolio of generation facilities in Alberta with approximately 2 gigawatts of capacity, participating in Alberta's competitive wholesale electricity market administered by the Alberta Electric System Operator (AESO). We actively participate in Alberta's energy-only market, optimizing our generation assets and managing market positions to deliver reliable electricity to the province.

## Our Team

Operating from Calgary, our team leverages deep market knowledge and operational expertise to navigate Alberta's dynamic electricity market while supporting Capital Power's broader commercial operations across North America.
