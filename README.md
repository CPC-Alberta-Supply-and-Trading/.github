# Capital Power Alberta Supply and Trading

## About Us
Capital Power Supply and Trading operates as part of Capital Power Corporation (TSX: CPX), a growth-oriented North American power producer headquartered in Edmonton, Alberta.

## Our Alberta Operations
We manage a diverse portfolio of generation facilities in Alberta with approximately 2 gigawatts of capacity, participating in Alberta's competitive wholesale electricity market administered by the Alberta Electric System Operator (AESO).
Our Alberta facilities include natural gas-fired generation and renewable energy assets supporting the province's electricity grid.
